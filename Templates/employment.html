Employment Information

In the United States, there are many ways to find jobs. Below is a list of sources that can be helpful in finding a job in the US, as well as give you information about the job from people who work there.


Employment Resources

Job Portals

  • Monster
  • Glassdoor
  • Indeed - Job search website by location that allows keywords such as “Foreign Worker”
  • Optnation - For international students
  • Immigrant Welcome Center

For job advice, CareerBuilder and LinkedIn are great sources.
