Education

In the United States, the education system is rather similar to other nations. Beginning with elementary school,
which many children begin at age 6, students progress through grades kindergarten to 5th grade, and then move on to secondary school.
Secondary school is usually split between middle and high school, with students graduating in the 12th grade. There are many choice for
secondary and other schools, with students choosing to go to advanced programs, religious schools, private schools, public (state-run) schools,
and charter schools. After secondary school, there are many colleges and universities that provide the opportunity to gain a degree and to further
expand your studies. Here you can find information on the different schools and how to register for these schools.
